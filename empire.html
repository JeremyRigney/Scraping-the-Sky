The Empire State Building is a 102-story skyscraper located in Midtown Manhattan, New York City, on Fifth Avenue between West 33rd and 34th Streets.
3,400 workers constructed the 102 storey building in just 410 days, a record build time. Construction started on January 22, 1930, and was completed on May 1, 1931.
The Empire State Building was built at a cost of $24,718,000. $40,948,900 if land is included.
