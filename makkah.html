CONSTRUCTION
The skyscraper officially opened in 2012 after construction began in 2004. The 76 flors make it one of the tallest skyscrapers in the world. It also has the largest and tallest clock face in the world. An observation deck was made 560 meters up to allow a 360 degree view of Mecca.

DID YOU KNOW?
The Clock Tower is topped by a four-faced clock, visible from 25 kilometres away. The clock is the highest in the world at over 400 metres above the ground. Each of the faces measure 46 m in diameter and are illuminated by 2 million LED lights. Just above the clock alongside huge Arabic script reading: “God is the Greatest” on the north and south faces and on the west and east the Koran.
