CONSTRUCTION
One World Trade Center refers to the main building of the new World Trade Center complex in Lower Manhattan, New York City. It is the tallest skyscraper in the Western Hemisphere, and the fourth-tallest in the world.
The construction began on April 27, 2006 and the building officially opened on November 3, 2014.
ARCHITECTURE
Address:1 World Trade Center, New York, NY 10006, United States
Owner: Emaar Properties
Architects: Daniel Libeskind, David Childs
Height: 417 meters, 546 m including the spire
The building has 104 floors and has a Modern architecture and Contemporary architecture style.
DID YOU KNOW?
The building is 1,776 feet tall (plus spire), to represent the year the declaration of Independance was signed. The first 57 meters of the building are made reinforced blast resistant concrete. The rest of the exterior concrete is 1 meter thick.
